Coding Challenge

Instructions

Enter a number to find out if it's divisible by 3, divisible by 5, or by both, then press the button and the numbers divisible by 3 will appear as "Soda", and the numbers divisible by 5 will appear as "Pop", the numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 will appear as "Soda Pop." You can also change the number values for "Soda" and "Pop" to divide your number by two new values.
